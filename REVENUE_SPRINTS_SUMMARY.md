## The Five Sprints

### R1: Lead Research (2 days, Mar 19-20)
Find 50 best companies from 6,524 database using pain-level scoring. SQL → LinkedIn enrichment → pain segmentation.
- **Output:** 50 qualified leads with emails + pain scores
- **Effort:** 12 hours
- **Owner:** PM (with engineer for SQL)

### R2: Deck & Pricing (2 days, Mar 21-22)
Launch pricing page (3 tiers: 5K/12K/25K), write pitch deck, build minimal employer dashboard.
- **Output:** Pricing live, dashboard works, deck ready
- **Effort:** 10 hours (code + design)
- **Owner:** Engineer + Designer

### R3: Personal Outreach (2 days, Mar 23-24)
Тим personally emails 50 companies with personalized templates (A/B/C by pain). Target: 2-3 demo calls booked.
- **Output:** 50 emails sent, 2-3 demos booked
- **Effort:** 15 hours (mostly Тим)
- **Owner:** Тим
- **Success metric:** 25%+ open rate, 5%+ reply rate, 50%+ demo conversion

### R4: Pilots & Brand (3 days, Mar 25-27)
Process first 1-2 paid deals (manual invoices). Launch @mcphire_insights TG channel (500+ target). Build viral calculator.
- **Output:** 1-2 paid customers, TG channel, calculator live
- **Effort:** 15 hours
- **Owner:** Тим + Engineer (calculator)

### R5: Scale to 15 (2 days, Mar 28-29)
Automation setup (Lemlist), testimonial collection, final pushes → 15 paying customers, 150K₽ MRR.
- **Output:** 15 customers, 150K₽ MRR
- **Effort:** 10 hours
- **Owner:** Тим + Engineer (automation)

---

## Why This Works

1. **Targeted outreach** (not spray-and-pray): 50 companies with HIGH pain = 10%+ conversion potential
2. **Personal touch** (not automation): Cold email + human conversation = higher close rate
3. **Multiple channels** (not one-shot): Email + TG channel + calculator = diversified leads
4. **Fast feedback loop**: Improve pitch based on replies (day 5), not guessing
5. **Social proof compounding**: First 3 customers → easier to get next 12
